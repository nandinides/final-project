Beloved Book Cover Redesign | Graphic Design

For this project I redesigned the book cover for one of my favorite books of all time.

I redesigned the book cover of one of my favorite books, Beloved by Toni Morrison. I made a simple flat illustration to incorporate some of the novelâ€™s themes. Drawing attention to the illustration in the center, I kept the text pretty simple with a calligraphy font for the book's title and a thin font for the author's name and writing on the back cover. I stuck with a brown color scheme, but contrasted the dark brown statue with the light brown background. I added texture to the background with stain-like splashes of color, contrasting with the flat textureless illustration.

size: front - 5.5 in x 8.5 in, binding - 1 in x 8.5 in, back - 5.5 in x 8.5 in
