Hi! I'm Nandini Desai, and I'm a freshman majoring in Cognitive Science with an emphasis on Design and Interaction at the University of California, San Diego. I'm a digital creator focused on branding, cultural identity, and sustainable design. I've long been interested in designing for myself and others from freelance to commissioned work. I aim to bridge the human experience in my art and other aspects of my life. This website is an embodiment of who I am as a designer and will continue to be updated with what's to come in my journey.
